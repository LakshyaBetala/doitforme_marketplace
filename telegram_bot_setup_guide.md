# 🤖 DoItForMe BotFather Setup Guide

This guide tells you exactly what to send to `@BotFather` on Telegram to make your Bot look professional.

## 1. Edit Name (Optional)
*Command to send to BotFather:* `/setname`
*Select your bot:* `@doitforme_alerts_bot`
**What to enter:**
```text
DoItForMe Alerts
```

## 2. Edit About (This shows on the bot's profile page)
*Command to send to BotFather:* `/setabouttext`
*Select your bot:* `@doitforme_alerts_bot`
**What to enter:**
```text
Official notification bot for DoItForMe. Stay updated instantly when you receive gig applications, new messages, or matching gigs! 

Link your account at doitforme.in/profile
```

## 3. Edit Description (This shows when users open the chat for the first time before clicking "Start")
*Command to send to BotFather:* `/setdescription`
*Select your bot:* `@doitforme_alerts_bot`
**What to enter:**
```text
Welcome to the DoItForMe Notification Center! 🚀

This bot connects securely to your DoItForMe account to provide instant, real-time alerts.

You will receive notifications for:
💬 Direct Messages
📄 New Gig Applications
🎉 Hired / Offer Accepted Alerts
🎯 New Gigs matching your Preferences

To get started, click the "Connect Telegram" button on your DoItForMe Profile page!
```

## 4. Edit Bot Picture (Profile Icon)
*Command to send to BotFather:* `/setuserpic`
*Select your bot:* `@doitforme_alerts_bot`
**What to enter:** 
*Upload the main DoItForMe Logo (the purple sloth or your primary brand icon) as a Photo.*

## 5. Edit Commands
*Command to send to BotFather:* `/setcommands`
*Select your bot:* `@doitforme_alerts_bot`
**What to enter (copy and paste this exact block):**
```text
start - Connect your DoItForMe account to this bot
help - Get help and support links
profile - Go to your DoItForMe Profile
gigs - View your active Gigs
```

## 6. Privacy Policy (Required for Telegram Login / Verification later)
*Command to send to BotFather:* `/setprivacy`
*Select your bot:* `@doitforme_alerts_bot`
**What to enter:**
```text
https://doitforme.in/privacy-policy
```

---
**Done!** Your bot will now look highly professional, trustworthy, and native to the platform.
